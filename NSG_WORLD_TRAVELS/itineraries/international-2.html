Marvellous Singapore

04 Nights/05 Days stay at Singapore

  • Day 01

    Arrival and a Night Safari tour

    On Arrival in Singapore, our Representative will meet at the airport. Transfer to Hotel. Relax at the hotel. In the evening you will be taken for an exceptional and exciting trip to the world's first wildlife park - Night Safari.

  • Day 02

    Half-day City tour and Sentosa Island

    After breakfast, get ready for a pleasurable trip to the city. Visit Art’s Museum, Dhoby Ghaut, Helix Bridge, Merlion Statue, Singapore Flyer, Thian Hock Keng Temple, The Esplanade Theatres on the Bay and drive through Orchard Road. After the half-day city tour, you will visit Sentosa Island. Go on a cable car ride in Sentosa and enjoy panoramic views. You will be exploring attractions like Madame Tussauds and Wings of Time. Return to the hotel and relax overnight.

  • Day 03

    Singapore: Universal Studios

    After breakfast at the hotel, you will be taken to Universal Studios to “live” the movies. The region’s first Hollywood movie themed park, Universal Studios is a heaven for movie buffs. It houses attractions and offers movie-themed rides that are exclusive. Shows and rides are mostly based on superhit movies. Let Shrek and Gang take you to an incredible 4-D adventure in the world’s first Far Far Away Castle. If you love thrill, go to face warrior mummies on Revenge of the Mummy – a psychological thrill ride in darkness. Also visit Madagascar, New York, Hollywood, Sci Fi City, and The Lost World. After an amazing day, come back to the hotel and relax overnight.

  • Day 04

    Leisure Day

    The fourth day of the trip is a leisure day and you can plan it as per your convenience. After breakfast you can go for shopping. Overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 05

    Departure

    After breakfast at the hotel, check out from the hotel. Now, you will be transferred to the airport. Catch your flight back home with plenty of sweet memories of the trip.
