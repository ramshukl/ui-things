Magical Kashmir

04 Nights/05 Days Kashmir Tour

  • Day 01

    Arrive – Srinagar + Local Sightseeing

    Arrive at Srinagar Airport. Meeting on arrival and transfer to Hotel. Later, visit Mughal Garden "Nishat" (The Garden of Pleasure) and "Shalimar" (Abode of All). Drive along the Lake side on Boulevard. Upon return, visit a local Handicrafts Emporium. After lunch, visit Shankaracharya Temple. In the evening, enjoy an Hour of Boat Ride on Dal Lake by a gondola Boat called ”SHIKARA” witness the ride on lake, called Venice of India & experience the aquatic life of Srinagar. Overnight at the Deluxe Houseboat

  • Day 02

    Srinagar – Sonamarg – Srinagar

    After breakfast, leave for full day excursion trip to Sonamarg. Situated at an altitude of 9000 Ft. Sonamarg – 84 Kms is also called as “The meadow of gold”. Ponies can be hired for the trip up to Thajiwas glacier, which is a major local attraction during the summer. Return for Dinner & overnight stay at the Hotel.

  • Day 03

    Srinagar – Gulmarg – Srinagar

    Full Day return excursion to Gulmarg, called as “Meadow of Flowers”. Gulmarg is 53 Kms from Srinagar at an altitude of 8700-ft. Gulmarg also referred as Meadow of Flowers is a famous Ski Resort & the world’s highest 18 hole Golf Course. Return to Srinagar for Dinner & Overnight at the Hotel.

  • Day 04

    Srinagar – Pahalgam – Srinagar

    After breakfast, proceed for a day trip to Pahalgam, called as “Valley of Shepherds. In Pahalgam, enjoy the nature & walk around the banks of River Lidder. Pahalgam is famous for various trekking routes and serves as the base camp for Amarnath Pilgrimage. Return back to Srinagar for overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 05

    Departure

    Departure from Srinagar Airport to board the flight for onwards destination. TOUR ENDS WITH SWEET MEMORIES!
